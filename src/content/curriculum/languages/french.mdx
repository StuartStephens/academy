## Why study French?

French remains an important language in diplomacy, literature, education, and international communication. It offers access to both a major modern language community and a large cultural tradition.

## Core areas

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Pronunciation | Sound-spelling relationships and liaison require attention |
| Verb forms | Tense and agreement matter for fluent expression |
| Gender and articles | Noun systems affect nearly every sentence |
| Register | Formal vs informal usage affects tone and correctness |
| Reading and listening | French often differs significantly between written and spoken form |

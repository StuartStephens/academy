## Why study English deliberately?

Even for native speakers, English benefits from structured study. Grammar, rhetoric, tone, and clear writing improve both academic and practical communication.

## Core areas

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Grammar | Supports clear and consistent sentence construction |
| Vocabulary | Expands precision and nuance |
| Reading comprehension | Improves interpretation and analytical ability |
| Writing style | Shapes clarity, persuasion, and tone |
| Speaking and listening | Critical for real-world communication |

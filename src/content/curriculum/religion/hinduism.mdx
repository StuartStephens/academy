## Core context

Hinduism is a diverse family of traditions rather than a single uniform system. It includes many philosophies, ritual forms, stories, and devotional practices.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Diversity of tradition | Belief and practice vary widely across communities |
| Dharma and duty | Ethical and social life is often framed through duty and order |
| Ritual and devotion | Worship, pilgrimage, festivals, and home practices are central |
| Philosophy | Hindu thought includes multiple schools and concepts of reality and liberation |

## Important study areas

1. Vedas, epics, and later sacred texts.
2. Major deities and devotional traditions.
3. Karma, dharma, moksha, and rebirth.
4. Temple, household, and festival practice.

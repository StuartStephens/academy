## Core context

Christianity developed from the life, teachings, death, and resurrection claims concerning Jesus, and expanded into diverse global traditions over time.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Jesus and salvation | Central to Christian theology and identity |
| Scripture | Old and New Testaments shape teaching and practice |
| Church traditions | Catholic, Orthodox, and Protestant traditions differ in structure and doctrine |
| Global expansion | Christianity became a major world religion across many cultures |

## Important study areas

1. Jesus and the early church.
2. Biblical canon and interpretation.
3. Sacraments, worship, and theology.
4. Major historical branches and global diversity.

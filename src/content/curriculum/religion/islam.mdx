## Core context

Islam is a monotheistic tradition centered on revelation to the Prophet Muhammad, the Quran, and a way of life shaped by worship, ethics, law, and community.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Revelation | The Quran is central to Islamic belief and practice |
| Practice | Prayer, fasting, charity, and pilgrimage structure Muslim life |
| Law and ethics | Islamic legal and moral traditions influence everyday conduct |
| Diversity | Muslim communities vary across regions, schools, and histories |

## Important study areas

1. Quran and prophetic tradition.
2. Five Pillars and devotional practice.
3. Sunni, Shia, and broader interpretive diversity.
4. Historical expansion and modern global Islam.

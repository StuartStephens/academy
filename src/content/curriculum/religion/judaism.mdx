## Core context

Judaism is one of the world's oldest monotheistic traditions. It is rooted in covenant, scripture, law, memory, and communal life.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Covenant | Relationship between God and the people of Israel |
| Torah and scripture | Foundational texts shape law and identity |
| Ritual practice | Sabbath, festivals, prayer, and dietary rules structure life |
| Historical continuity | Jewish life has persisted across diaspora, persecution, and renewal |

## Important study areas

1. Hebrew Bible and Torah.
2. Rabbinic tradition and interpretation.
3. Ritual life and holy days.
4. Diaspora history and modern diversity.

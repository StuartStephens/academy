## Cars

Cars include paint, clear coat, trim, glass, leather, screens, and rubber. Treating all of those materials the same is a common way to cause damage.

### Safer process

1. Rinse loose grit first.
2. Wash with automotive soap.
3. Use clean microfiber tools and separate dirty areas from paintwork.
4. Dry with soft towels.
5. Use material-specific products for glass, interior trim, or leather.

## Glassware

For drinking glasses, dishes, and decorative glass, use warm water, mild soap, and soft cloths or sponges.

### Avoid

- Abrasives on fine or coated glass.
- Sudden temperature shock.
- Letting mineral-heavy water dry on the surface if clarity matters.

## Windows and flat glass

- Use lint-free cloths or dedicated glass towels.
- Avoid overspray near electronics, wood trim, and porous surfaces.
- Dry and buff after cleaning to reduce streaking.

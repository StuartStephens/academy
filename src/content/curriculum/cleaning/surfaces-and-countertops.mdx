## Match the cleaner to the surface

Countertops and work surfaces are not interchangeable. Acidic, abrasive, or over-wet cleaning can permanently dull, stain, swell, or scratch the material.

## Common cautions

| Surface type | Common caution |
| --- | --- |
| Natural stone | Avoid acidic cleaners unless specifically compatible |
| Wood | Avoid standing moisture and aggressive soaking |
| Laminate | Avoid over-saturating seams and edges |
| Stainless steel | Avoid harsh scratching abrasives |
| Quartz/engineered surfaces | Avoid unnecessary harsh chemicals and heat assumptions |

## Safer method

1. Remove loose crumbs and grit.
2. Use a mild cleaner suited to the surface.
3. Wipe with a soft cloth.
4. Dry after cleaning when moisture can sit or wick.

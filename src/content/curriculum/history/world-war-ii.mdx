## Core context

World War II emerged from unresolved consequences of World War I, expansionist regimes, economic instability, and aggressive authoritarian politics.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Total war | Entire economies and societies were mobilized |
| Fascism and authoritarianism | Ideology shaped war aims and repression |
| Holocaust and genocide | Mass murder became central to the war's moral history |
| Postwar order | The UN, superpower rivalry, and reconstruction followed |

## Key turning points

1. Axis expansion in Europe and Asia.
2. Major early victories by Germany and Japan.
3. Allied industrial mobilization and strategic coordination.
4. Turning points in Europe and the Pacific.
5. Allied victory and postwar restructuring.

## Long-term impact

World War II transformed global power, weakened old empires, elevated the United States and Soviet Union, and shaped the geopolitical order of the late twentieth century.

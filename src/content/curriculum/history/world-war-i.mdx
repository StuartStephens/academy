## Core context

World War I began in a Europe shaped by militarism, alliance blocs, imperial rivalry, and nationalist tensions. A regional crisis escalated into a global industrial war.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Alliance systems | Local conflict widened into continental war |
| Industrial warfare | Technology increased scale of destruction |
| Trench warfare | Stalemate defined much of the Western Front |
| Imperial collapse | Old empires fell and political maps changed |

## Key turning points

1. Assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand.
2. Mobilization of alliances and major powers.
3. Prolonged trench warfare and attrition.
4. US entry into the war.
5. Armistice and postwar treaty settlement.

## Long-term impact

The war destroyed empires, redrew borders, and created political instability that contributed to later conflicts, including World War II.

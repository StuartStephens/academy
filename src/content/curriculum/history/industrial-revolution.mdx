## Core context

The Industrial Revolution transformed production through mechanization, new energy systems, factory organization, and expanding transportation networks.

## Major themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Mechanization | Production shifted from small-scale craft to industrial processes |
| Urbanization | Population and labor moved toward industrial cities |
| Labor change | Work became more regimented, specialized, and often exploitative |
| Global impact | Industrial capacity changed imperial power and warfare |

## Key turning points

1. Growth of mechanized textile and factory production.
2. Expansion of coal, steam power, and transportation networks.
3. Urban-industrial social transformation.
4. Labor conflict and reform responses.
5. Spread of industrial systems beyond Britain.

## Long-term impact

Industrialization changed class structure, state capacity, military power, and the pace of social and economic life across the modern world.

## Why group these together?

Automotive, marine, and aircraft maintenance all involve mechanical systems, inspection discipline, troubleshooting, and repair standards. They differ in environment and regulation, but they share a strong maintenance mindset.

## Shared themes

| Theme | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Diagnostics | Correct troubleshooting prevents guess-repair work |
| Preventive maintenance | Scheduled care avoids larger failure cascades |
| Safety | Mechanical systems can fail dangerously if ignored |
| Documentation | Good records improve reliability and accountability |

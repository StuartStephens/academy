## Why farming matters

Farming is the foundation of food production and a major force shaping land use, economies, ecosystems, and social organization.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Soil health | Soil quality affects yields, resilience, and long-term sustainability |
| Water management | Irrigation, drainage, and rainfall timing shape productivity |
| Crop systems | Rotation, diversity, and timing matter for health and output |
| Livestock systems | Animal health, feed, and land use affect farm operations |
| Economics and logistics | Farming depends on markets, labor, storage, and transport |

## Main farming questions

1. What is being grown or raised?
2. What inputs are required?
3. What environmental risks affect the operation?
4. How do soil, climate, and water interact?

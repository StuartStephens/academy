## What fans do

Fans move air to improve circulation, ventilation, cooling, or comfort. They generally prioritize volume of airflow more than strong pressure buildup.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Blade design | Influences airflow pattern, noise, and efficiency |
| Motor type | Affects power use, speed control, and durability |
| Housing and guard design | Shapes airflow path and safety |
| Use case | Ceiling, box, axial, and computer fans serve different needs |
| Noise and vibration | Important for real-world usability |

## Common tradeoffs

- Airflow volume vs noise.
- Compact size vs efficiency.
- Fixed speed simplicity vs variable control.

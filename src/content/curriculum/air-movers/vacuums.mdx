## What makes a vacuum work?

A vacuum system creates a pressure difference so air moves through an intake path, carrying dust and debris into a collection and filtration system.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Pressure difference | Drives airflow through the system |
| Filtration | Affects air quality, clogging, and motor protection |
| Brush and intake design | Changes pickup effectiveness by surface type |
| Motor and airflow path | Strongly affect suction behavior |
| Maintenance | Full bins, clogged filters, and worn seals reduce performance |

## Key maintenance checks

1. Empty or replace collection bags/bins.
2. Clean or replace filters.
3. Inspect hoses and seals for leaks.
4. Remove hair and debris from moving parts.

## Why study bicycles?

Bicycles combine mechanics, materials, ergonomics, maintenance, and efficiency in a highly accessible system. They are a strong way to learn practical engineering and mechanical reasoning.

## What this section covers

| Topic | Core focus |
| --- | --- |
| Design | Geometry, fit, handling, and component choices |
| Maintenance | Inspection, adjustment, wear, and repair routines |
| Engineering | Loads, materials, drivetrain behavior, and performance tradeoffs |

## Shared principles

1. Fit affects safety, comfort, and performance.
2. Maintenance prevents larger mechanical failures.
3. Design choices always involve tradeoffs.
4. Materials and load paths matter more than appearance.

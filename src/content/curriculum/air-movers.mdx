## Why study airflow devices?

Vacuums, fans, and blowers all move air, but they do so for different purposes and with different pressure, flow, and system design priorities.

## What this section covers

| Topic | Core focus |
| --- | --- |
| Vacuums | Suction, debris handling, filtration, and pressure drop |
| Fans | Air circulation, cooling, and ventilation flow |
| Blowers | Directed airflow and higher-pressure movement |

## Shared principles

1. Airflow and pressure are related but not identical.
2. Efficiency depends on housing, motor, and blade/impeller design.
3. Noise, vibration, and heat affect usability and performance.
4. Filters and resistance change system behavior significantly.

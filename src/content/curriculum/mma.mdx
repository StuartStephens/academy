## What is MMA?

Mixed martial arts combines striking, grappling, clinch work, takedowns, and ground fighting into a single competitive rule set. It rewards technical integration, conditioning, and tactical decision-making.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Striking | Distance, timing, and damage potential shape exchanges |
| Grappling | Takedowns, control, and submissions change positional dynamics |
| Clinch | A transitional range with major strategic value |
| Conditioning | Cardio, recovery, and durability affect performance deeply |
| Game planning | Opponent-specific tactics matter at every level |

## Training areas

1. Striking fundamentals.
2. Wrestling and takedown defense.
3. Brazilian jiu-jitsu and positional control.
4. Conditioning and injury prevention.
5. Sparring and strategic adaptation.

<Alert tone="warning" title="Training safety">
Combat sports training carries real injury risk. Technical instruction, controlled progression, and qualified coaching matter.
</Alert>

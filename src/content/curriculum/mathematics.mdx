## Why study mathematics?

Mathematics trains precision, abstraction, logic, and quantitative reasoning. It helps people model patterns, make decisions, and understand relationships in science, engineering, economics, and everyday life.

## What this section covers

| Area | Core focus |
| --- | --- |
| Arithmetic | Number sense, operations, fractions, ratios, and everyday calculation |
| Algebra | Variables, equations, functions, and symbolic reasoning |
| Geometry | Shapes, measurement, proof ideas, and spatial relationships |
| Calculus | Change, limits, rates, accumulation, and continuous models |

## Shared study principles

1. Practice beats passive reading.
2. Show steps clearly so errors are visible.
3. Learn concepts and procedures together.
4. Build from smaller patterns before harder abstractions.

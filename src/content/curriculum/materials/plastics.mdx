## What are plastics?

Plastics are polymer-based materials that can be engineered for flexibility, rigidity, transparency, insulation, chemical resistance, and low-cost manufacturing.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Polymer type | Different plastics behave very differently |
| Thermoplastics vs thermosets | Processing and reuse depend on chemistry |
| Manufacturing | Injection molding, extrusion, and forming change product design |
| Durability | UV, heat, impact, and chemical exposure affect lifespan |
| Environmental tradeoffs | Waste, recycling limits, and product life cycles matter |

## Common applications

- Packaging
- Electronics housings
- Medical devices
- Consumer goods
- Automotive parts

## What is paper?

Paper is a sheet material made primarily from plant fibers processed into a bonded network. Its properties depend on fiber type, density, coating, moisture, and intended use.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Fiber source | Affects strength, texture, and cost |
| Pulping and processing | Determines cleanliness, durability, and surface behavior |
| Coatings and finish | Affect print quality, water resistance, and feel |
| Weight and thickness | Important for packaging, printing, and handling |
| Moisture sensitivity | Paper changes behavior with humidity and liquid exposure |

## Common uses

- Writing and printing
- Packaging and shipping
- Filtration and specialty products
- Art and archival work

## What is concrete?

Concrete is a composite material made from cement, water, aggregates, and often additives or reinforcements. Its performance depends heavily on mix quality, curing, placement, and environmental exposure.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Cement chemistry | Hydration drives hardening and strength gain |
| Aggregate selection | Size and composition affect strength and workability |
| Water ratio | Too much or too little water changes performance |
| Curing | Proper curing improves durability and structural quality |
| Reinforcement | Steel and other reinforcement help manage tension loads |

## Common uses

- Foundations
- Slabs and sidewalks
- Structural frames
- Roads and infrastructure

## Why this category needs care

Sushi and sashimi depend on ingredient quality, freshness, precision, and careful handling. Rice preparation, knife work, and temperature control matter as much as the seafood itself.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Rice preparation | Sushi rice texture and seasoning are foundational |
| Seafood quality | Handling standards strongly affect outcome and safety |
| Knife work | Clean cuts preserve texture and presentation |
| Temperature control | Important for quality and food safety |
| Species differences | Shrimp, oysters, and mackerel all require different handling |

## Requested examples

- **Shrimp**: often cooked, chilled, and prepared carefully for texture.
- **Oysters**: freshness, shucking skill, and storage matter heavily.
- **Mackerel**: strong flavor and delicate handling make preparation style important.

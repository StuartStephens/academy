## Why alcohol fits into food and beverage study

Alcohol production is closely tied to fermentation, flavor development, ingredient choice, and beverage handling. Beer, wine, and spirits differ in process, raw materials, and sensory outcome.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Fermentation | Converts sugars into alcohol and flavor compounds |
| Distillation | Separates and concentrates alcohol in spirits production |
| Ingredient base | Grain, fruit, sugar sources, and botanicals shape the final beverage |
| Aging and storage | Time and container type can change character significantly |
| Service and handling | Temperature, glassware, and oxidation affect perception |

## Major beverage groups

1. Beer.
2. Wine.
3. Spirits.
4. Fermented specialty beverages.

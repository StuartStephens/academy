## Why eggs are worth studying

Eggs are simple ingredients that reveal technique quickly. Small changes in heat, timing, and fat use dramatically change the outcome.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Heat control | Eggs toughen easily if overheated |
| Texture targets | Scrambled, fried, poached, and boiled eggs all require different handling |
| Freshness | Affects poaching, peeling, and flavor |
| Fat and seasoning | Small changes affect richness and finish |
| Timing | Seconds matter more with eggs than with many foods |

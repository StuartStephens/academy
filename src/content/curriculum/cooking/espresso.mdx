## Why espresso is technical

Espresso is a concentrated extraction process where grind, dose, water, pressure, temperature, and time all affect the cup quickly.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Grind size | Too coarse or too fine changes flow and taste dramatically |
| Dose and yield | Ratio strongly affects balance and body |
| Extraction time | Helps diagnose under- or over-extraction |
| Water and temperature | Affect consistency and flavor clarity |
| Workflow | Distribution, tamping, and cleanliness improve repeatability |

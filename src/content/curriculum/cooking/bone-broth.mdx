## What makes bone broth different

Bone broth relies on long extraction from bones, connective tissue, and aromatics. Time, temperature, and clarity management all affect the final result.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Bone selection | Different bones contribute flavor, fat, and body differently |
| Roasting | Can deepen flavor before simmering |
| Simmer control | Gentle simmering usually gives cleaner broth than hard boiling |
| Skimming | Helps reduce surface impurities and cloudiness |
| Straining and storage | Important for safety and usability |

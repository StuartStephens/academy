## Why rice matters

Rice is one of the world’s most important staple foods, but good rice depends on grain choice, water management, and heat control.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Grain type | Long-grain, medium-grain, and short-grain rice cook differently |
| Water ratio | Strongly affects final texture |
| Rinsing | Can affect starchiness and separation |
| Resting after cooking | Steam redistribution improves consistency |
| Cooking method | Absorption, steaming, and rice-cooker methods differ |

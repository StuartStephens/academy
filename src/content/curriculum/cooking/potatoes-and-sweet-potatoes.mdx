## Why potatoes are technique-sensitive

Potatoes and sweet potatoes behave differently because of differences in starch, sugar, moisture, and structure. Method strongly affects texture.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Starch behavior | Changes crispness, mash texture, and structure |
| Roasting | Surface dryness and heat affect browning |
| Boiling and steaming | Useful for mash and softer textures |
| Sweetness and moisture | Sweet potatoes behave differently from standard potatoes |
| Cut size | Uniformity affects even cooking |

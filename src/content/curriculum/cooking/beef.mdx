## Why beef needs different approaches

Different beef cuts behave differently based on fat content, muscle use, and connective tissue. Some cuts want fast high heat, while others improve through slow cooking.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Cut type | Tender and tough cuts need different methods |
| Connective tissue | Slow cooking can transform tougher cuts |
| Fat content | Affects flavor, moisture, and browning |
| Cooking method | Grill, roast, braise, and sear all suit different cuts |
| Resting and slicing | Affects tenderness and perceived juiciness |

## Crop categories matter

These crops do not behave the same. Some are perennial trees, some are herbaceous tropical crops, some are vining annuals, and coffee behaves more like a specialty perennial plantation crop.

## Quick crop comparison

| Crop | Main farming considerations |
| --- | --- |
| Strawberries | High disease pressure, soil management, intensive harvest timing |
| Blueberries | Acidic soil, drainage, pruning, and cultivar choice |
| Mangoes | Warm climate, tree spacing, disease management, fruit handling |
| Bananas | Tropical water and wind management, disease risk, sucker management |
| Coconuts | Coastal/tropical climate fit, long-term tree maintenance |
| Pineapples | Tropical/subtropical conditions, drainage, spacing, weed control |
| Watermelon | Warm season, pollination, irrigation timing, fruit rot prevention |
| Cantaloupe | Similar to melon systems with strong disease and moisture management needs |
| Coffee | Elevation/climate fit, shade systems, processing quality, labor intensity |
| Tamarind | Long-lived tree crop, climate fit, pruning, orchard planning |

## Crop-specific notes

### Strawberries

- Often high-input and high-attention.
- Require strong weed, moisture, and disease control.
- Post-harvest handling is critical because fruit is fragile.

### Blueberries

- Soil acidity is one of the most important variables.
- Drainage and root-zone conditions matter heavily.
- Long-term pruning and plant structure affect yield quality.

### Mangoes

- Need climate fit and orchard planning.
- Pest, disease, and fruit-drop management matter.
- Harvest timing affects quality and storage.

### Bananas

- Water, nutrition, and wind exposure are major concerns.
- Disease pressure can be severe depending on region.
- Plantation management is very different from backyard growing.

### Coconuts

- Long-term tropical crop with site-specific viability.
- Tree access, storm exposure, and harvest logistics matter.

### Pineapples

- Prefer good drainage and careful weed control.
- Often grown in systems where uniformity matters.

### Watermelon and cantaloupe

- Need warm growing conditions.
- Irrigation timing, soil warming, disease control, and pollination are key.

### Coffee

- Crop quality depends on climate, elevation, processing, and labor.
- Specialty coffee systems often care as much about post-harvest handling as cultivation.

### Tamarind

- Functions more like a long-term orchard tree.
- Climate and long-term land-use planning matter more than quick turnover.

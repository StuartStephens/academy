## Why plumbing matters

Plumbing is part of the basic infrastructure of health, sanitation, and building function. Reliable water delivery and waste removal are fundamental to safe homes, hospitals, schools, and cities.

## Core plumbing topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Supply lines | Deliver clean water to fixtures and systems |
| Drain, waste, and vent systems | Remove wastewater and protect indoor sanitation |
| Fixtures and fittings | Connect people to water use and drainage |
| Pressure and flow | Affect performance, comfort, and reliability |
| Leak prevention | Poor sealing or installation can cause major structural damage |

## Core system areas

1. Cold and hot water distribution.
2. Drainage and wastewater flow.
3. Venting and pressure balance.
4. Pipe materials and connection methods.
5. Maintenance and inspection.

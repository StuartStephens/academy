## Why geometry matters

Geometry develops spatial thinking and precise reasoning about shape, distance, angle, area, and structure. It matters in design, construction, engineering, mapping, and proof-based thinking.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Shapes and figures | Build recognition of basic geometric structure |
| Angles and measurement | Support layout, construction, and analysis |
| Area and volume | Connect geometry to practical physical quantities |
| Congruence and similarity | Show when shapes match or scale in predictable ways |
| Coordinate geometry | Links algebra and geometry together |

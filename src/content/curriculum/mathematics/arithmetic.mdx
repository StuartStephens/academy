## Why arithmetic matters

Arithmetic is the foundation of nearly all later mathematics. It supports budgeting, measurement, estimation, data interpretation, and basic problem solving.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Whole-number operations | Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division underpin everything else |
| Fractions and decimals | Essential for precision and comparison |
| Ratios and proportions | Used in measurement, scaling, and real-world quantities |
| Percentages | Common in finance, statistics, and daily decisions |
| Estimation | Helps catch mistakes and reason quickly |

## Why calculus matters

Calculus provides a way to reason about continuous change. It is central in physics, engineering, economics, biology, and any field that models growth, motion, rates, or accumulation.

## Core topics

| Topic | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Limits | Describe what functions approach near a point |
| Derivatives | Measure rates of change and slope |
| Integrals | Measure accumulation, area, and total change |
| Applications | Connect calculus to motion, optimization, and systems |
| Interpretation | Meaning matters as much as symbolic computation |
